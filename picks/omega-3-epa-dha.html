Why we picked this omega-3

Editorial pick from the Suvo team.

The product

Igennus High-Quality Omega-3 — a UK-formulated fish oil widely available across the EU. The 2-softgel daily serving provides 670 mg EPA + 330 mg DHA (1000 mg combined) in re-esterified triglyceride form — the structure with the best absorption data — plus 1 mg astaxanthin as a natural antioxidant. We look at EPA + DHA combined because that is the number with evidence behind it, not the larger “fish oil mg” figure printed on the front of most labels.

What we looked at

  • EPA + DHA dose per serving (the only number that actually matters).
  • Triglyceride vs. ethyl-ester form.
  • Third-party purity and oxidation testing.
  • Price per gram of combined EPA + DHA (not price per softgel).

How to take it

With a meal that contains fat. Omega-3s are fat-soluble and absorb roughly three times better when taken with food versus on an empty stomach.
