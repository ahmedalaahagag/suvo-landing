Delete your Suvo account

Last updated: May 2026

This page explains how to delete your Suvo account and all data associated with it. Suvo is published by Suvo (developer name on Google Play and the App Store).

Option 1 — Delete from inside the app (recommended)

  1. Open the Suvo app and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings → Your data.
  3. (Optional) Tap Export my data first to download a JSON file of everything we hold about you.
  4. Tap Delete my account and confirm. Your account and all associated data are erased immediately and the action cannot be undone.

Option 2 — Email us

If you cannot access the app, send an email from the address registered on your Suvo account to hello@getsuvo.com with the subject line "Delete my Suvo account". We will verify your identity by replying to that address and will delete your account within 30 days of a verified request.

What gets deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your email address and account record.
  • Your supplement stack (every supplement you added, with brands, doses, and schedules).
  • Your intake log (every "taken" event, every reminder acknowledgement).
  • Any label photos you uploaded to our moderation queue that are attributed to your account.
  • Your reminder settings and push-notification tokens.
  • Your in-app preferences (streak counters, onboarding state).

What is retained (and why)

After your account is deleted, we retain a small amount of data for the reasons below. None of this retained data is linked to your email address or to any identifier that can be traced back to you.

  • Anonymised, aggregate server logs — request counts, error rates, and performance metrics. Retained for up to 90 days for operational and security purposes, then automatically deleted. These logs do not contain your email, your stack, or your intake history.
  • Catalog contributions — if you contributed a new supplement barcode or submitted a label photo that was accepted into our public supplement catalog, the catalog entry itself remains (the product exists and benefits other users), but the attribution to your account is removed. We retain this indefinitely because it is product data, not personal data.
  • Backup snapshots — our database is backed up for disaster recovery. Residual copies of your data may exist in backups for up to 35 days after deletion, after which they are automatically overwritten. Backups are encrypted and are not accessed for any purpose other than disaster recovery.

Questions

For anything about this process, email hello@getsuvo.com. We read every message.
